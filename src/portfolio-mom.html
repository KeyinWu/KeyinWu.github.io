Mom's Coming Home

  • VirtualReality

  • C#, Unity3D, Oculus

  • Targy Feng, Keyin Wu, Yancan Wang, Steve Goehrig

  • Sep, 2022

Mom's Coming Home is about a boy who really likes ice cream. He wants to focus on homework before his mom gets home so that he won't be punished by mom and also won't lose the ice cream. The storytelling is largely dependent on sounds. This game based on Oculus Quest 2 is a two-week project in Building Virtual Worlds course.

Contribution

Gameplay Programming (Game Manager, Event Manager...), Game Design (Game ending, Interaction Format, Play Experience), Supportive 3D Modeling (Room Decoration)
